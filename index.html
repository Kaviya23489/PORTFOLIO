About the analyst

2022–2026 batch, B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science. I'm looking to join an early-stage AI or data-driven startup where one person's work is still visible in the outcome.

As a Data Analyst, my job is to turn ambiguous, messy datasets into a clean narrative: SQL to interrogate, Python to shape, Power BI or Tableau to make it click for someone who has 90 seconds to look at it. That means faster, more confident decisions for whoever I'm building for — and fewer hours lost to spreadsheets that don't tell a straight story.

I'm also here to keep growing as an analyst — sharper SQL, better dashboard design, more instinct for which number actually matters. Outside of dashboards, I'm deep into music, Indian culture, and where AI meets creative and business decision-making — which is why my flagship project is a lifecycle study of what actually makes a song stay on the charts, not just enter them.
